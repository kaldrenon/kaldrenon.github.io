```
how exactly do i explain
  that time stands still
  when it flies by like that
  
  what words
convey
  a deeply lackadaisical
    sprint
      toward sunset

it's a baffling thing
  how time becomes an illusion
  when your attention
  is the only thing
  to have my attention
```
