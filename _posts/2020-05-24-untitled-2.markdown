```
here i am again
at the unfamiliar place
  i remember so well

    sharp curves and smooth angles
  bright shadows
dim sunbeams

  everything
    turned
    on its head
    for balance

i have absolutely no idea what i'm doing
  and i've never felt
  more confident
```
